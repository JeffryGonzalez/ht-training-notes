## Event-Driven Architecture
This three-day course will prepare students to design, develop, and deploy a services architecture based on Event-Driven architectural practices.
Developers will utilize Apache Kafka as the backbone of their application.
### Prerequisites
This course is designed to be a "graduate" level course for developers that have taken all the Hypertheory Training Services courses.
